UB Hacking 2023

Phishing is a very cost-effective attack that doesn't take much time to create. In fact the UBHacking website took no longer than 10 minutes to imitate.


People with all levels of technological experience are vulnerable to phishing.


Here are some tips to avoid getting phished:

• Always check the source of the link, are they reputable? Are they trying to impersonate a reputable person/organization?

• If you do fall for the phishing email, immediately change all accounts that use the same passwords

• Always look for mistakes on the website. Phishing attacks can be more successful when targets are not looking close enough.
